EX10

Write a script to calculate the BMI (Body Mass Index) of a person, the formula is as follows

BMI (Body Mass Index): BMI = mass (kg) / (height(m) x height(m)) Note: you must do the conversion from cm to m before calculation

And then based on the calculated BMI, tell them that they are: Severely underweight if BMI < 16 Underweight if BMI is between 16 and 18.5 Normal if BMI is between 18.5 and 25 Overweight if BMI is between 25 and 30 Obese if BMI is more than 30
